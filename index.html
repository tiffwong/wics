About

Our purpose is to support, recruit, and retain women pursuing a degree in the broad field of computing. WiCS will enhance communication among women in computing through informal dinners and gatherings, speakers, field trips, and arrange for tutoring and mentoring for its members as needed. The ultimate goal is to empower women in computer fields to succeed and thrive in the Drexel community and beyond.

WiCS is primarily a social group located at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA. We explore methods for the retention of women in our computing programs while having a fun time. We are an official student chapter of ACM-W.
